Hello! I'm Jane, a trilingual designer and developer fueled by a passion for the arts and character design. I thrive on bringing creative ideas to life.

My journey began with a deep appreciation for Japanese culture in childhood, which inspired me to study the Japanese language during high school. After earning my bachelor's degree in graphic design from an international university in Thailand, I took the bold step to further my studies in Japan, specializing in character design. This transformative experience immersed me in a unique cultural environment, deepening my understanding of design principles and techniques, and honing my skills for a successful career.

As I gain experience in website development, I am excited to explore new opportunities that allow me to showcase my abilities and make a meaningful impact. I aspire to blend my diverse knowledge to elevate the websites I create, making them engaging and accessible for all users. My goal is to incorporate innovative design elements and user-friendly features that resonate with a wide audience, ensuring each site is not only visually captivating but also easy to navigate.
